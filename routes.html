ROUTE 1 BUENOS AIRES

Day 1: Buenos Aires - Recoleta and Downtown

Morning: Arrival in Buenos Aires. Explore the Recoleta neighborhood. Visit the famous Recoleta Cemetery, where historical figures like Eva Perón are buried.

Afternoon: Stroll along Avenida 9 de Julio to see the Obelisco and the Teatro Colón. Stop at Café Tortoni for a traditional coffee.

Evening: Dinner in the Puerto Madero neighborhood, known for its cuisine and river views.
