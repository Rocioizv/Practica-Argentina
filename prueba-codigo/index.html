How to Get to Argentina

Here are some travel suggestions from major cities around the world:

  • From New York: Fly to Ministro Pistarini International Airport (Ezeiza), Buenos Aires (Approx. 10-11 hour flight).
  • From London: Fly to Ministro Pistarini International Airport (Ezeiza), Buenos Aires (Approx. 13-hour flight).
  • From Paris: Fly to Ministro Pistarini International Airport (Ezeiza), Buenos Aires (Approx. 14-hour flight).
  • From Sydney: Fly to Ministro Pistarini International Airport (Ezeiza), Buenos Aires (Approx. 22-hour flight with a stopover).
  • From Tokyo: Fly to Ministro Pistarini International Airport (Ezeiza), Buenos Aires (Approx. 23-hour flight with a stopover).
